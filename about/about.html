About Us

Welcome to El Amor Huy Anh, where the vibrant flavors of Mexico come to life in Vietnam. We are passionate about serving authentic Mexican cuisine made with love. Our fresh ingredients, unique recipes, and hash quesadillas all move with the fresh ingredients. Our cozy atmosphere and friendly staff welcome you to experience the taste of Mexico. Whether you're at a casual meal, join us at El Amor Huy Anh for a delightful culinary experience that brings the heart of Mexico to you.

Our Story

At El Amor Huy Anh, our journey began with a passionate love for Mexican cuisine. Founded by a group of food enthusiasts who wanted to bring authentic Mexican flavors to Vietnam, our team is bringing unique and bold tastes to the local dining scene. Through extensive travel and research in Mexico, we've dedicated ourselves to sourcing the freshest ingredients and learning traditional recipes to create dishes that are bursting with flavor. Each dish we serve tells a story, inviting you to explore the culinary heritage of Mexico.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our guests with the best authentic Mexican dining experience, served with a touch of creativity. We are committed to using only the finest ingredients possible while creating a warm and friendly atmosphere where everyone can enjoy meals with family and friends. We strive to create unforgettable and memorable moments for every guest who walks through our doors.
